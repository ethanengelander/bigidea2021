Shawarma

Usually enjoyed wrapped in Laffa, Shawarma is a meat dish combined with an assortment of spreads and vegetables.

Falafel

A popular street food that consists of fried balls of ground chickpeas stuffed into a pita and is served with a variety of veggies and spreads.

Hummus

A paste of pureed chickpeas usually mixed with sesame oil or sesame paste and eaten as a dip or sandwich spread.
