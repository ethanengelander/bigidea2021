 Hummus

 Hummus is a dip or spread made from chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic.

 Falafel

 Falafel is a fried spice ball or patty of ground chickpeas or fava beans (or a mixture of both).

 Shakshouka

 Shakshouka is a dish of eggs poached in a sauce of tomatoes, olive oil, peppers, onion and garlic.

 Israeli Salad

 Israeli salad is a chopped salad of finely diced tomato, onion, cucumber, and bell or chili peppers. It's a standard accompaniment to many Israeli meals.

 Shawarma

 Shawarma is meat cut into thin slices; it can be made from lamb, chicken, or beef.

 Medjool Dates

 Dates are a type of palm fruit that are grown in large quantities in Israel. They are sold as individual fruits or in other various forms like a spread.
